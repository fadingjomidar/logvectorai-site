How it works

1 Ingest

Pull Oracle alert.log, listener logs, AWR extracts, and OCI metrics. Normalize and enrich with host, DB, and tenant metadata.

2 Embed

Use a transformer encoder on GPU to generate dense vectors for each event; keep tokenized context for LLM explanation.

3 Index

Store vectors in OCI AI Vector Search or FAISS/Milvus; enable fast k‑NN queries for look‑alike detection.

4 Detect

Cluster, score outliers, and track drift. When risk spikes, we enrich alerts with similar past incidents and fixes.

5 Forecast

Train lightweight time‑series models on waits/CPU/I/O to predict saturation windows and recommended actions.
